Two decoys, two jobs

Both plant something fake in a resource you own. A tracer wants an AI to absorb and repeat the fact, so you can detect a leak. A context bomb wants an AI to refuse and abandon, so you can deter an attack. They must never share an artefact — pick the tool for the job.
